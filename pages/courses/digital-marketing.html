Digital Marketing

Professional skill-based training at SOS Technical Training Institute, Karachi.

Course Overview

The Digital Marketing course is designed to transform students into proficient online growth experts. In an era where businesses are moving online, these skills are highly sought after. This 6-month program covers the full spectrum of digital strategies—from Search Engine Optimization (SEO) to Social Media Marketing and Paid Advertising. Whether you want to join a global agency or start your own freelancing business, this course provides the tools and tactical knowledge to succeed in the modern economy.

What You Will Learn

  • Digital Marketing Fundamentals & Fundamentals
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) - On-page & Off-page
  • Social Media Marketing (SMM) & Brand Management
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC) & Google Ads
  • Content Marketing & Creative Strategy
  • Google Analytics & Performance Tracking
  • Freelancing Skills & Client Handling
  • Professional Workplace Ethics & Rights
